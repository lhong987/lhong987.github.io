Colophon

Website designed in Sketch, coded in Sublime Text on a Mac and powered by Jekyll. The build process is automated thanks to Gulp and Git makes deployments worry-free.

Webfonts are from Typekit and include JAF Bernino Sans and FF Meta Serif Web Pro. The picture for this about page was shot by the one and only Yves Druart.
